Array's Project Leadership Strategy

  • Effective Team Communication
  • Regular and thorough internal and joint team meetings
  • Status updates, data review meetings, and efficient use of face-to-face meetings
  • Team-wide use of centralized, consolidated status trackers and issue/resolution trackers
  • Prepare a project plan with team contacts, communication lines, overall timelines
  • Develop Realistic Timelines
  • Develop comprehensive and detailed timelines for project activities
  • Assign ownership and link dependencies to all tasks, large and small
  • Monitor timelines and progress of each pending task, large and small
  • Anticipate Risk, Prevent Delays, Avoid Extra Costs
  • Use timelines and issue trackers to identify at-risk milestones early, while there is still time to remedy
  • Involve all key reviewers at early draft stages of study to avoid re-work and delays
  • Establish a clear path to final sign-offs of study documents and statistical results
  • Proper Resource Management
  • Apply the art and science of resourcing in a cost-effective and efficient manner
  • Establish a streamlined, dedicated team to achieve efficiency and consistency, just large enough to meet established goals and deadlines with a small buffer
  • Have a back-up resourcing team available to quickly respond to unanticipated extra work and/or aggressive timelines
